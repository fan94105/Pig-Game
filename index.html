Game Rules

  • Players take turns rolling a six-sided die.
  • Roll the die to accumulate points or hold their current points and pass the turn to the next player.
  • If a player rolls a 1, they lose all their accumulated points for that turn and their turn ends.
  • If a player rolls any other number, they can choose to roll again or hold their points and end their turn.
  • The points accumulated on each turn are added to the player's overall score.
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.
